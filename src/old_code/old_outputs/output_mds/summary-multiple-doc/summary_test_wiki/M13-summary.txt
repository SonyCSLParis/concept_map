The Geopolitical Constitution: Executive Expediency and Executive Agreements Joel R. Paul University of California Hastings College of the Law California Law Review, No. 86, July 1998 Abstract:     From the Founding through the Second World War well established understandings constrained executive power over foreign relations. Paul challenges the continued reliance on Cold War discourse and offers an alternative approach to adjudicating questions on the reach of executive foreign relations power.
