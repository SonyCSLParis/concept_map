Besides the temple visit , there are other activities you can do in Borobudur and one of activities is watching a dance performance `` Sendratari Mahakarya Borobudur `` ( Borobudur 's Great Work of Art Dance ) that describes how Borobudur 's construction was done more than a millennia ago . Located at Aksobla theater inside the Borobudur complex , the performance involves more than 150 men and women . You need not travel to Thailand or India for the ride , as you can have that at Borobudur too .
