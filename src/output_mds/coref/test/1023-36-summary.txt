The disproportionate targeting of students with disabilities shows that , in addition to schoolwide approaches , many schools include specific strategies for dealing with bullying in the IEPs of students with disabilities . The Massachusetts Aggression Reduction Center gives these suggestions ( reprinted with permission ) for ways parents can support parents child who is being bullied : Tell your child that this is not child or child fault , and that your child did nothing wrong .
