Denial of Direct PLUS Loans based on adverse credit In this section , we explain why some parents or graduate students who were previously denied Federal PLUS Loans by FFEL Program lenders may be approved for Direct PLUS Loans . Awarding additional Direct Unsubsidized Loan funds to dependent students In this section we remind schools of schools authority to award additional Direct Unsubsidized Loan funds to a dependent undergraduate student based on a determination that the student 's parent is unable to borrow a Direct PLUS Loan due to having an adverse credit history ( see the preceding section of this letter ) or other exceptional circumstances .
