The complaints procedure in schools If your child is being bullied at school , the following procedures should be followed : First of all , talk to your child 's teacher to see if teacher are aware of the situation . This leaflet gives you tips on preventing and tackling bullying , signs to look out for if you suspect your child is being bullied , how to deal with cyberbullying and prejudice-driven bullying as well as advice on what to do if your child is doing the bullying .
