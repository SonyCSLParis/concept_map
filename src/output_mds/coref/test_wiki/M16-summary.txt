It began 9 years earlier in July 1972 , when Political Prisoner Status was introduced after a hunger strike by 40 IRA prisoners led by Billy Mc Kee . By 1980 , Status was decided on another hunger strike . Three days later the prisoners were allowed the right to wear prisoners own clothes and one by one all the demands were met , but to save face , the Brits refused to officially recognize the Kee as political prisoners .
