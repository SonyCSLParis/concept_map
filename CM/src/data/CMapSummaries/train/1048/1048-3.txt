Parenting Tips on Potty Training
How potty charts really help parents of toddlers or preschoolers is through consistency and the easy-to-see tracking of potty training success. Our parenting articles provide useful feedback and tips on how to potty train a toddler or young child, potty charts , and how to deal with potty training regression. You'll learn how to make using the potty fun for kids, and how to keep your child potty trained as they head off to preschool and kindergarten. Learning how to potty train is a process for parents that can quickly become frustrating, so patience and consistency are key.
